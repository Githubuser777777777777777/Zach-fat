Poem

Zach’s got moves, he’s hard to track, When he walks by, he needs a bigger crack. He’s got a snack stash that’s off the chart, The fridge shakes when he makes a start. He’s not just big, he’s a legend in size, When he squeezes in chairs, they wince in surprise! But Zach’s got heart, and that’s no joke— Even his shadow needs a snack and a Coke!.
